Teaching Methodology

  • The classes start with the 11th standard topics in the beginning and then conclude with the 12th standard topics.
  • The course gets completed by the end of February and special doubt removal sessions are conducted based on the All India Mock Test Series.
  • Teaching consists of theory coupled with important concepts and fundamentals. Formulae are taught through solved/unsolved examples/illustrations and derivations.
  • The Discussion part is more of interactive type and students are motivated to raise and discuss their doubts/objections to understand the concepts/fundamentals used in problem solving.
  • 60% of the lecture is concentrated on school syllabus and 40% will be helping the student to solve question based on the base concept from JEE (Main & Advance). (Click here to see a Sample Test Analysis Report )
  • The faculty members follow standardized lecture notes while delivering their lecture. This helps in synchronized teaching in all the batches across the course.
  • Appropriate home work is assigned in the form of Practice Problems, Problems from the Sheet and Problems from study material.
  • The assigned home work is discussed in the next class with interactive participation of all the students
  • Regular online tests are conducted for performance assessment.

    • What is AskIITians?

    AskIITians was started with the concept of helping students who leave the comfort of their homes and live in difficult circumstances to prepare for JEE, AIPMT and other Engineering and Medical entrance Examinations. The unique Model of AskIITians brings the classroom to the student’s desktop. AskIITians, through its various programs like live online classroom coaching, study material, discussion forum, test series, classroom recordings and many more, ensures that students get an ex-IITian/ Doctors to share their toil and guide them in every step while the students are sitting in their own homes. AskIITians employs around 90+ ex-IITians and 32+ top rank experts from premier medical institute who help students in preparing for IIT JEE/AIPMT, mentoring them in tough times; helping them decide the streams they should choose, boost their morale and instill the confidence that an ordinary average student can also crack IIT JEE, AIPMT and other engineering and medical exams In a way these ex-IITians/Doctors at AskIITians acts not only as a teacher, but as a mentor, a companion, a well-wisher and acounselor.
