JEE Advanced 2015 Application Procedure

JEE Advanced 2015 Online Registrations will start from 2nd May 2015 to 7th May 2015.

Check out the eligibility criteria for JEE Advanced 2015 before applying for the exam! 

You can find all the details of the JEE Advanced 2015 Application Form Procedure in the information brochure as well as on the official exam website. We bring you all the main points that you have to keep in mind while submitting application for JEE Advanced 2015:

JEE Advanced 2015 Registration Process

  • Create a User ID and Password to log on the website.

  • Fill in the particulars in the exam form, which will include: 

    • Your choice of language for the JEE Advanced question paper,

    •  Up to three cities with exam centers (which should fall under same Zonal IIT region),

    •  Whether it is their first attempt or second attempt to crack IIT JEE,

    • Whether they belong to DS category and other such details.

  • Students will also be asked whether they need a scribe. Visually impaired candidates who cannot read questions or candidates who do not have fingers or hands and cannot darken bubbles in the ORS sheet may need a scribe.

  • Candidates will need to upload the scanned copies of the following:

    • Original passport-size photograph in JPEG format,

    • Legible signature (which should not be in capital letters) in JPEG format,

    • Date of Birth Certificate,

    • Class X Certificate, and

    • Category and Sub-Category (PwD) Certificate.

    • Once the form is submitted a Bank Challan is generated which is to be used to pay the exam fees as mentioned below.

JEE Advanced 2015 Application Fees 

  • All female students are exempted from paying the registration fees for JEE Advanced 2015, except for those who choose Dubai as their exam center.

  • General category male students will have to pay Rs 2,000 to sit for the exam while SC, ST and PwD candidates will have to pay Rs 1,000 as the application fees.

  • All candidates appearing for the IIT JEE exam from Dubai will have to pay US$ 220, irrespective of their category or gender.

JEE Advanced 2015 Payment Mode

  • Students will have to take the print out of the Bank Challan generated when they fill the application form and use it to pay the fees at the any State Bank of India (SBI) branch which has the facility of Core Banking Solutions (CBS).

  • The bank will endorse the counterpart of the Bank Challan once the fee is paid and you will need to keep it safe as a receipt.

  • The deadline to pay JEE Advanced application fees is May 7, 2015 by 5 pm. 

  • Only those candidates who register for JEE Advanced 2015 and pay its fees can download their JEE Advanced 2015 Admit Card.

  • Once students apply for the exam, they cannot withdraw from it. No claim of refund of fees is entertained in any case.
