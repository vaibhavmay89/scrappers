The Himachal Pradesh Board of Secondary Education

Exam Name: The Himachal Pradesh Board of Secondary Education

Exam Category: Secondary Level

Conducting Organization: Himachal Pradesh Board

Introduction:

The Himachal Pradesh Board of School Education, Dharamshala came into existence in 1969. Besides conducting the examinations for class 10th and 12th, the board also stipulates syllabus, courses and text books for school education in Himachal Pradesh.

Some of the key features of the Himachal Pradesh Board of School Education are:

  • The Board conducts examination for the following classes and courses: 10, 10+2, J.B.T and T.T.C

  • Around 5 Lakh students appear the Himachal Pradesh Board exams annually.

  • The board constitutes around 8000 affiliated schools

  • There are around 1650 examination centers throughout the state

  • Apart from a Liaison Office at Shimla, there are around 19 Sale Book Depots/Information Centers in Himachal Pradesh to cater to the needs of students.

The board overall responsibilities include:

  • Prescribing relevant syllabi and text books to the students studying under Himachal Pradesh Board

  • Fairly and timely conducting of examinations and declaration of results

Himachal Pradesh Board Class 10 Important Dates

Himachal Pradesh Board class 10 exams were conducted from March 6, 2014 to March 20, 2014 at around 1650 examination centers all over the state.  This year, around 10,12,581 appeared the Himachal Pradesh Board exam in which 5,35,662 were boys and 4,76,919 were girls.
