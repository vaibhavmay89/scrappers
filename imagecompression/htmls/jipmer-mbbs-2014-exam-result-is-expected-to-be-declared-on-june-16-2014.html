JIPMER MBBS 2014 Exam Result is Expected To Be Declared On June 16, 2014

The expected result date for JIPMER MBBS entrance examination 2014 is likely to be declared on June 16, 2014.  The examination was conducted on June 8, 2014 at 376 centers across 36 cities in India.

Students can download their marks/rank card from the official website once the result is declared.

The test centre of the MBBS 2014 entrance examination was distributed across 7 cities in the northern zone, 17 cities in the south, 8 cities in the east and 4 cities in the west.

The minimum percentile required for qualifying the JIPMER MBBS 2014 Exam is 50% for unreserved candidates, 45% for unreserved candidates of (OPH) and 40% for reserved candidates of SC/ST/OBC.

The total number of seat allotted for the JIPMER MBBS 2014 exam was 150. A growth of about 35% was noticed in the number of registration as compared to last year.
