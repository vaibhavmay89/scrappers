KIITEE MEDICAL 2014 ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

The candidate appearing the KIITEE MEDICAL 2014 must have completed the 10+2 from an institution as per the CBSE or an equivalent examination with a minimum score of 50% aggregate in Biology, Chemistry and Physics subjects. The candidates also need to have cleared the English subject paper. For the SC/ST Candidates 40% aggregate marks in Biology, Chemistry and Physics all together.

Age Limit: The candidates appearing the Entrance test must be of at least 17 years and not more than 25 years as on 31st of December 2014. For the SC/ST Candidates the upper age limit may be relaxed by 5 years.

The application form for the KIITEE MEDICAL 2014 will be available online only and the candidates have to apply by visiting the website: www.kiitee.ac.in or www.kiit.ac.in and they can download the application form.

SYLLABUS & PATTERN FOR KIITEE MEDICAL 2014

Syllabus

The syllabus for the KIITEE MEDICAL 2014 consists of the following subjects:

  • Physics: Measurement, Motion, Work, Energy and Power, Oscillations, Gravitation, Waves, Thermodynamics, Electromagnetic Induction, Electrons and Photons,

  • Chemistry: Atomic structure, Equilibrium, elements, Solid state Chemistry, Redox reactions, Chemical bonding, Chemical thermodynamics, Hydrogen, Chemical kinetics, Electrochemistry, Hydrocarbons, carbon compounds, Organic compounds, Polymers, Environmental Chemistry, Bimolecular Compounds.

  • Biology: Living World, Genetics and Evolution, Reproduction, Growth and Movement in Plants, Reproduction and Development in Humans, Ecology and Environment, Structure and Function Animals, Biotechnology and its Applications, Biology and Human Welfare.

Exam Pattern

A total of 120 questions from 3 subjects (Physics Chemistry and Biology) are to be asked in the KIITEE MEDICAL 2014. Each subject carries 45 question each from Physics and Chemistry, and 90 questions from Biology. Each question carries four marks and with every wrong answer one negative mark is also deducted. The duration for the exam is set to 3 hours.

Tips & Tricks for KIITEE MEDICAL 2014 Preparation

The entrance exams conducted by KIIT University, Bhubaneswar for the Medical courses are one of the reputed degree programmes of East India. Candidates appearing for the exam need to prepare thoroughly for the exam as there are always a tough competition among the Medical seats.

  • Prepare Study Plan: Planning is the key. A proper study plan can lead you in having a zero panic during the exam. The thorough study of the topics in the syllabus and ignoring the pert not mentioned in the syllabus can do great for the candidates appearing the exam.

  • Past Year Question: Going through last year’s questions and preparing thoroughly keeping them in mind cal guide the candidates in a better direction than preparing roughly from the textbooks.

  • Sample Papers: Now-a-days there are plethoras of sample papers available in the market prepared by intellectuals working in the educational field for years. Pick up any of them and start practicing the questions and find out the area of weakness of yours. This can let you have an idea where you need to work more and where you can take things easy.

  • Stay cool, take breaks: It is obvious that the exam pressure can be too much exhaustive, and hence it is suggested that after regular intervals, candidates need to take a break while preparing for the exam. This keeps the mind cool and recharges the energy stored. Also take sound sleep at night and start early in the morning.

COUNSELING

As per the notification for the institution, 100 MBBS and 100 BDS seats are there in the Kalinga Institute of Medical Sciences (KIMS) which is a constituent of the KIIT University, Bhubaneswar. The candidates scoring marks above cutoffs will be sent intimation for the counseling process which is to be commenced from June 2nd 2014 and will be carried on till June 15th 2014. Based on the merit list the selected candidates will be called for the counseling at the Bhubaneswar campus.

Documents to be carried by the candidates on the date of Counseling:

  • Rank Card downloaded from the website www.kiitee.ac.in

  • Original mark sheets/Certificates along with the photocopies of class 10th Board and 12th Board Examination

  • Admit Card of KIITEE MEDICAL 2014

  • Two passport size photographs

  • Demand Draft or INR 62,000/- which also includes the counseling fees of INR 6000/- in the favor of “KIIT”, payable at Bhubaneswar. The cash payment will not be accepted.

Either of the parent or the guardian can attend the counseling on the behalf of the candidate with the documents such as the ID proof of the candidate, Authorization letter from the candidate, Certificate mentioning the name of the parents/guardian (10th Pass certificate/birth certificate).
