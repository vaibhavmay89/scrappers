EAMCET Result: Fruits of your labour!

While preparing for EAMCET exam seems boring and the actual day of the exam is dreaded, one cannot just wait to know their EAMCET result and their rank in the exam. Even askIITians tutors remember the day their exam results were declared quite fondly. Did you know all askIITians tutors have firsthand experience of cracking IIT JEE, EAMCET and other prestigious competitive entrance exams?

EAMCET exam will be conducted on May 17, 2014 and EAMCET results will be declared on June 2, 2014.

The result of the exam will not be communicated to students personally. Students will have to check it online.

Students are advised to print out their EAMCET rank card and keep it safe until the admission process gets over. They will need to show it during counselling and other steps that follow it.

How EAMCET 2014 Rank will be calculated? 

  • To calculate ranks, 75% weightage is given to EAMCET marks and 25% weightage is given to marks in the qualifying exam.
  • EAMCET ranks are then allotted to students on merit-basis.

EAMCET counselling dates have not been announced yet. You should check this page from time to time to check out EAMCET counselling results as soon as they are announced.
