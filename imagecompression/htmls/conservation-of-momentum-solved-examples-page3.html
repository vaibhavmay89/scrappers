Solved Examples 

Example 5

         A bullet of mass 'm' kg is fired with a speed of u m/sec. from a freely hanging gun of mass M. Calculate the recoil velocity of the gun.

Solution:

        Pi = Pf

        0 = m.u + Mv

                v = - mu/M.

 

Example 6

        An 8 gm bullet is fired horizontally into a 9 kg block of wood and sticks in it. The block, which is free to move, has a velocity of 40 m/sec. after the impact. Find the velocity of the bullet. Also calculate heat dissipated in the collision.

Solution:

        For bullet & block system

                        Pin = Pf

                mu + M.0 = (m + M)v

                or     u = (m+M)v/m

                                = (0.008+9)0.40/0.008

                                = 450 m/sec

        Heat dissipated = loss in K.E.

                                = (1/2) mu2 - (1/2) (M + m)V2

                                = (1/2) 0.008 (450)2 - (1/2) (9 + .008) (0.4)2

                                = 809.3 J.
