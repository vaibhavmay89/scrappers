ABOUT the institute

The national Institute of Technology, Goa, also known as NITG is one of the newly formed NITs in the country by the Government of India. Founded in the year 2010 NITG commenced its first batch in 2010-11. NITG offers B.Tech degrees in various disciplines and the admission procedure is same as that of other NITs in the country. JEE ranks are the factor deciding the admission into the NITG. 50% of reservations for the students of Goa and remaining seats are open for students across the country. Students at NITG involve in various technical activities along with the cultural activities as well. As per the present batch of students, there are a total of 298 students enrolled in the B.Tech programmes at NIT Goa.

NIT Goa offers UG and PG courses in various Engineering disciplines; Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical and Electronics engineering and Electronics and Communication Engineering. The institute also offers Ph.D in all these domains as well along with Ph.D in Mathematics, Physics, Economics, Chemistry, Mechanical Engineering, and English is also offered by the institute. The admission process of NITG is on the basis of the ranks scored in the JEE Mains for the Indian students and has a 50% of reservation for the Goan students as well. As per the DASA (Direct Admission to Students Abroad) an intake of 30 foreign students is also allowed by the institute.
