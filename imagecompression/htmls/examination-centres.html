List of JEE Main Examination Centers for 2014

Jee Main Examination Centers are situated in almost all the major cities and towns across India as well as abroad – in cities and countries with higher concentration of Indian students aspiring to study engineering in their homeland.

If students are going to appear for Pen & Paper based examination, they have to choose four JEE Main Exam Centers 2014 while filling up the JEE Main application form. Students can state them in the order of their preference. Efforts will be made to allot centers according to the students’ choice but they may be assigned a different city nearby if it is not possible.

For students who are going to appear for Computer based examination, they just have to choose one of the JEE Main Entrance Exam Centers 2014 from the given list. However, if number of students registering for a computer based test in a particular city is less than 50, the examination center will be struck off the list and the candidates will be allotted a center in the nearby city.

Tips to Choose an Examination

  • Candidates who opt to write both Paper 1 and Paper 2 might have to choose exam centers for both offline and online exams as Paper 2 is conducted only in offline mode.

  • Make sure that the exam centers you choose are easily accessible to you.

  • For the offline exam, you will have to choose four exam centers. Choose them in the order of your preferenc.

  • If you need directions, call the Exam Center a day before the exam so that you do not reach late for your exam.
