10. A curve C has the property that if the tangent drawn at any point P on Cmeet the co-ordinate axes at A and B, then P is the mid-point of AB. The curve passes through the point (1, 1). Determine the equation of the curve.

11. Three players A, B and C, toss a coin cyclically in that order (that is A, B, C, A, B, C, A, B, ………) till a head shows. Let p be the probability that the coin shows a head. Let a, b  and y be, respectively, the probability that A, B and C gets the first head. Prov that  b = (1 – p) a .         Determine a, b  and y (in terms of p).

12. Prove , by vector methods or otherwise, that the point of intersection of the diagonals of a trapezium lies on the line passing through the mid-points of the parallel sides. (You may assume that the trapezium is not a parallelogram.)
