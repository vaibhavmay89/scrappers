Medical and Dental Admission Counseling, COMEDK UGET 2014 will Start From 3rd July

COMEDK UGET 2014 regarding the first round of dental/medical admission counselling will be held on 3rd, 4th and 5th of July 2014. There will be around 7 sessions altogether on each of the days. Students appearing for this admission will need to attend the sessions corresponding to the rank secured. The admission for dental/medical counselling course for ranks 1-2250 will be conducted on the 3rd of July. Again, the counselling course for ranks 2252-4500 and ranks 4501-23749 will be held on the 4th and 5th of July respectively.

Candidates who have appeared for the dental and medical stream entrance exam received their ranking as well as the scores according to their performance in some specific subjects like Chemistry, Physics and Biology. In the English subject candidates just need to score the qualifying marks. Overall, 25 dental and 14 medical colleges will conduct the admissions according to the COMEDK UGET 2014 scores.

The COMEDK, Consortium of Medical, Engineering and Dental Colleges of Karnataka undergraduate entrance test 2014 is generally conducted for granting admissions to the Dental, Medical, Engineering and Architecture undergraduate courses in the Karnataka state. The entrance exam is segregated into 3 categories. The first category is meant for students applying for the engineering courses, the second one is for students appearing for the dental and medical courses, and the third category is for students who want to apply for dental and medical, engineering courses. Each of the question papers includes multiple-choice questions of 1 mark each. There is no negative marking and the course is fully based on the Grade XII syllabus.
