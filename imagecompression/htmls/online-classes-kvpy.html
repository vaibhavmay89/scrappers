What is KVPY?

Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana (KVPY) is an ambitious scheme of the Department of Science and Technology (DST), Government of India to attract and nurture young talent to pursue education and research career in science. This ongoing National Program of Fellowships in Basic Sciences offers generous fellowship and contingency grant to selected KVPY fellows from Bachelor level up to the pre-Ph.D. level.

Besides, selected students are also invited to exclusive summer campus organized in some of the best and most prestigious research and educational institutions in the country.

Fellowships on Offer:

  • Students of B.Sc., B.S., B.Stat., B.Math.,  as well as first to third year of Integrated M.Sc. or Integrated M.S. courses receive a monthly stipend of Rs 5,000 and annual contingency grant of Rs 20,000.

  • Students of M.Sc. or fourth to fifth year of Integrated M.Sc. or Integrated M.S. courses receive a monthly stipend of Rs 7,000 and annual contingency grant of Rs 28,000.

The annual renewal of fellowships depend on the academic performance of students in each academic year (at least 60% marks for general category students and at least 50% marks for reserved category students including SC, ST and PwD students).
