GGSIPU CET 2015 ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

  • Candidates must have cleared the class 10+2 board or equivalent qualifying exam with minimum aggregate of 55% in subjects Chemistry, Physics, and Mathematics all together

  • The candidates must have cleared each of the subjects separately in the last qualifying exam

  • English must have to be the compulsory subjects in the last qualifying exam

  • Candidates appearing the Class XII board exam 2015 are also eligible to appear for GGSIPU CET 2015

INDRAPRASTHA UNIVERSITY APPLICATION FORM 2015

  • Candidates can fill the IPU CET 2015 application form online by visiting the official website http://ipu.ac.in/

  • For online filling of form the candidates are also required to pay the Indraprastha University application form fees of Rs.750/- along with the bank transaction charges as applicable via debit/card or internet banking.

  • Candidates can also obtain the application form by sending a request to The Joint Registrar(Admissions) - Administrative Block, Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, Sector-16C, Dwarka, New Delhi-110078

  • Candidates can also obtain application form from selected Indian bank branches and are required to fill them as required with attached documents and send them to Controller of Examinations, Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, Sector-16 C, Dwarka, New Delhi-110078

GGSIPU ADMIT CARD 2015

Candidates are required to visit the official website of GGSIPU CET http://ipu.ac.in/ to download the IPU CET 2015 admit card. The university shall not be sending the admit cards to the candidates by any means and are required to avail from the website only.

GGSIPU CET 2015 PAPER PATTERN

  • The exam duration to be allotted shall be 2:30 hours

  • The question paper shall contain 150 questions from Chemistry, Physics and Mathematics

  • The question shall be of objective type with multiple choices

  • Each correct answer shall fetch the candidates 4 marks and every wrong attempt shall deduct 1 negative mark

  • The IPU CET 2015 shall be conducted in English only.

IPU CET COUNSELING 2015

  • IPU CET 2015 Counseling shall be commenced in the month of July on the prescribed dates.

  • Selected candidates are required to be present on the counseling date with the required documents in order to confirm the seats

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED DURING THE IPUCET 2015 COUNSELING

  • Print out copy of filled in application form

  • Rank/Score card of GGSIPU CET 2015

  • Birth certificate/10th board certificate

  • Class 12th and 10th mark sheets and certificates

  • Category certificate (if applicable)
